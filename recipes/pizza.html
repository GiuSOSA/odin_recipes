Pizza Recipe

Description

This is the most good and fast thing to eat when you are hungry

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Pomodoro
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella

How to do it

  1. Take the flour and make it like a bread
  2. Open the bread like a circle and make it like a ufo
  3. Cover it with pomodoro
  4. Put the mozzarella above the pomodoro
  5. bake and enjoy
