Trilece Recipe

Description

This is the best cake human being has created in earth.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Caramello
  • Flour
  • Pandispagna

How to do it

  1. Take the flower and make the pandispagna
  2. Mix the three differents milks
  3. Pour the milk on the pandispagna
  4. Make the caramello and pour it over it
  5. Leave in fridge and enjoy
